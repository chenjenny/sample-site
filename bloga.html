Why is traveling important for Annette?


Annette, is the author of Bucket List Adventures, co-owner of Sugo Trattoria, a serial adventurer and creator of the award winning travel blog, Bucket List Journey. Annette passion is checking the world off of her bucket list one experience at a time.


After making a promise to herself to never let fear make her decisions, she has been encouraging others to dream bigger, step out of their comfort zones, have a curiosity of the world and live their best life


From eating crickets in Thailand to going on safari in Africa to swimming with thousands of Jellyfish in Palau, Annette has traveled to over 40 countries, 5 continents and countless cities to find the hottest bucket list worthy experiences around the world
