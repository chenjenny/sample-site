A Little Adrift

Blogger: Shannon O’Donnell




Shannon’s A Little Adrift is one of the most widely read and popular travel blogs. She sets out on a journey towards knowledge and aspect. With a strong value on culture and food.


Shannon started to travel around the world in 2008, she shares stories of culture and social enterprises to give other traveler the information and advice they need to travel the world, Including how we can all make responsible tourism.


Shannon inspire her readers with stories and photography. She also share tips on how to plan round the world travel, what it reality to live outside the U.S., and how we can all make responsible tourism
